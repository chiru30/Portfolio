Chiranthana Rampura

M.Sc CS Graduate, CONCORDIA UNIVERSITY

I’m the kind of researcher who enjoys making smart systems behave sensibly in the real world—when the data is messy, and assumptions break down. I like AI because data doesn’t lie the way people sometimes do—it forces clarity. I’ve always had a creative mind, which is why research feels natural to me: I enjoy chasing ideas, testing them on different problem statements, and learning what actually holds up. At the same time, I keep a practical lens—I care about translating concepts into something that works in the real world, not just on a perfect dataset. And I don’t treat failures as dead ends: every failed case is a clue, and often the start of a new research direction.
